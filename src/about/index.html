About us

NLP Labs, LLC (DBA NLP, LLC) believes that the power of natural language processing (NLP) should be accessible to anyone. Too much of human knowledge is expressed in natural languages, from The Analects to The Bible, from Beowulf to social media posts. Whether someone can code should not become a burden to tap into the vast amount of knowledge in text. We want to empower anyone to do NLP without knowing coding nor positional encoding in Google's BERT. The new NLP experience should be very much like doing regression analyses in Microsoft Excel. We are the Excel for text!
